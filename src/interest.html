Sep 25, 2016 - July 08, 2019

Ultimate frisbee is one of the training sports in my high school. As the co-founder of class frisbee team, I designed training program and promoted our team on social medias. To learn more tactics, I kept watching and analyzing the competitions from American Ultimate Disc League.
